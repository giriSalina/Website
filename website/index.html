Our Story

Traditional Craftsmanship

For over three generations, our family in the rural village of Clayhaven has been creating beautiful, functional pottery that celebrates our heritage and connection to the earth.

Each mug is handcrafted using locally sourced clay and traditional techniques passed down through our ancestors. We believe in creating pieces that bring joy to everyday rituals.

Our mugs are more than just vessels; they are expressions of our culture, our values, and our commitment to sustainable craftsmanship.

Our Process

Hand Preparation

Clay is sourced locally and prepared by hand, ensuring the perfect consistency for throwing.

Wheel Throwing

Each mug is carefully shaped on the pottery wheel by our master artisans.

Glazing & Decorating

Unique glazes and patterns are applied using traditional techniques.

Kiln Firing

Mugs are fired in our wood-burning kiln at high temperatures for durability.
